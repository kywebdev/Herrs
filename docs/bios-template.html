About Us

Good people making good snacks for good times

As a company rooted in family since the beginning, we proudly continue to be family-owned and -operated.

Since 1946, we’ve been making exceptionally delicious snacks for our friends, communities and environment. You can always trust that you’re getting an exceptional snack that’s made by our family for yours to enjoy.

The Herr family

Spanning four generations, the Herr family continues to guide Herr Foods Inc. in the direction of family, community and quality products.

Ed Herr

President & CEO

As the current president, CEO and spokesman of Herr Foods, Ed Herr follows in his father’s footsteps of putting product quality and business integrity first.

View Bio

J.M. Herr

Executive Chairman

James M. (J.M.) Herr has worked in the family business for over 40 years. As the executive chairman, J.M. oversees the long-term health of his father’s snack company.

View Bio

James S. Herr

Founder

The man that started it all, Jim Herr founded our family’s snack company over 70 years ago, creating a Forever Good legacy of kindness, persistence and integrity.

View Bio

Over 70 years of making snacks that make people smile

James S. Herr broke away from his family’s business of raising hens to build up his very own snack company — one that continues to thrive, over 70 years later.
