  • Philadelphia Area

    Take 1-95 South to Route 322 West to Route 1 South. Exit Route 272 (Nottingham) and turn left. Travel for 50 yards, turn right onto Herr Drive and follow signs to the Herr's Snack Factory Tour. Approx. 1 hour.

    Directions

  • Harrisburg

    Route 283 East to route 30 East. Take the 222 South exit. Pickup route 272 South. At Wakefield remain on 272 South to Nottingham. Crossover route 1 then turn right into Herr Dr. Follow signs to the Factory Tour. Approx. 90 minutes.

    Directions

  • Lancaster

    Route 283 East to route 30 East. Take the 222 South exit. Pickup route 272 South. At Wakefield remain on 272 South to Nottingham. Crossover route 1 then turn right into Herr Dr. Follow signs to the Factory Tour. Approx. 45 minutes.

    Directions

  • Baltimore Area

    Take 1-95 North to the Route 272 North East, Maryland exit. Go north on Route 272 to the village of Nottingham. We are near the intersection of Rts. 272 and 1. Turn left onto Herr Drive and follow signs to the Herr's Snack Factory Tour. Approx. 1 hour.
